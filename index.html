Understanding COVID-19

From a Bioinformatics Perspective

This website details our research into the SARS-CoV-2 virus. Background information and history of coranaviruses can be found on the History page. Information about different strains of COVID and our sequence alignment results are on the Strains page. The Vaccines page provides an interactive data table detailing the current vaccine trials going on in COVID-19 research. The Algorithms page provides information on how our sequence alignment and phylogentic trees were computed. The Contributors page lists our team members and their contributions to this project, as well our sources.
